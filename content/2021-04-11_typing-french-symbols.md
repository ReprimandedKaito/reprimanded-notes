## Guillemets

_POINTING DOUBLE ANGLE QUOTATION MARK_.

- « / `&raquo;` (HTML) / &#[x00AB](https://util.unicode.org/UnicodeJsps/character.jsp?a=00AB);
    `\guillemotleft` (LaTeX)

- » / `&laquo;` (HTML) / &#[x00BB](https://util.unicode.org/UnicodeJsps/character.jsp?a=00BB);
    `\guillemotright`
